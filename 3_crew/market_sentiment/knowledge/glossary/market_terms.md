# Market Glossary

## Sentiment-Related Terms

### Short Squeeze

**Definition:** A rapid price increase caused by short sellers being forced to cover positions
**Sentiment Indicator:** Typically bullish short-term, but may signal overextension
**Related Terms:** gamma squeeze, squeeze play

### Fear, Uncertainty, and Doubt (FUD)

**Definition:** Negative sentiment spread to influence price action
**Sentiment Indicator:** Bearish; often associated with coordinated selling
**Related Terms:** panic selling, capitulation

### FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)

**Definition:** Emotional buying driven by rapid price appreciation
**Sentiment Indicator:** Extremely bullish; often precedes corrections
**Related Terms:** euphoria, irrational exuberance

## Volatility Terms

### Realized Volatility

**Definition:** Historical price movement measured as annualized standard deviation of returns
**Calculation:** σ = √(Σ(ri - μ)² / (n-1)) × √252
**Threshold:** > 40% considered high for equities

### Implied Volatility (IV)

**Definition:** Market's expectation of future volatility derived from options prices
**Interpretation:** High IV relative to RV suggests uncertainty or upcoming events
**Threshold:** IV/RV ratio > 1.5 signals elevated expectations
